College Education

Indiana Baptist College

Greenwood, Indiana

Summary

I began attending IBC in the fall of 2003. My focus of study was in Pastoral Theology.

The Pastoral Studies Major, in addition to intensive classroom study, included a one-on-one mentorship program with a local pastor of your choosing. Under the tutelage of my pastor, Pastor David Zempel of Martinsville Baptist Tabernacle, I learned the everyday responsibilities of being a pastor of a local church. He taught me how to preach the Bible to a congregation, as well as many other practical responsibilities of a pastor.

Of all the courses of study I took part in throughout my years at IBC, the most rewarding study was that of the Biblical Greek language. My instructor, John Krinke, was a caring, patient teacher, who even took time out of his personal life to help his students understand how to study the New Testament in the original language.

I graduated in the spring of 2008. I graduated with honors: Magna cum Laude, The Greek NT Award, and The Bible Proficiency Award.
